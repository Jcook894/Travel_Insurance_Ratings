TravelInsuranceRating.org - Review Guidelines

Your postings on this site may become public if it meets our basic guidelines. Overall our rules are simple; be polite, don’t be offensive or malicious, don’t break the law, don’t defame or slander, and don’t post advertising messages.

By posting a review you agree to abide by our Guidelines. We do not intend to censor messages based on the opinions expressed within posts, but we reserve the right to remove or modify posts at our discretion and without explanation.

Please note that:

  • Any review that encourages illegal intent will not be approved for posting and will be removed. Participants may not link, in any manner, to sites or images containing pornography, sexually explicit, gross violence, or are determined by us to be detrimental to the community.
  • Reviews can not contain abusive, vulgar, or derogatory language.
  • Any posts that contain ads or other form of advertising will be removed. Posts that contain advertising links to other sites will be deleted.
  • Opinions, advice and all other information expressed in your review are yours and yours alone, even if modified by us. Posting your review on the web site is not to be construed as our agreement with you or endorsing your point of view.

We reserve the right to modify and amend these terms at any time without notice. It is your responsibility to remain informed of our current policies.

Keep your comments to the point. Remember your review will be read by others so don't add personal or medical information that you don't want published.
